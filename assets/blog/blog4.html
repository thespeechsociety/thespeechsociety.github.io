Induction Event: Unveiling New Potential

Induction Event: Unveiling New Potential

  • Pulkit Kumar Verma
  • Dec 28, 2023

The dawn of a new academic year brings with it a sense of new beginnings, an opportunity for students to embark on a path of growth, discovery, and fellowship. The Speech Society aka TSS successfully coordinated an awe-inspiring “Induction Event” for excited first-year students at the heart of this enthusiasm. TSS set an amazing experience for them that welcomed these young minds into the fabric of college life with a blend of passion, creativity, and a commitment to establishing a dynamic college community.

The Induction Ceremony brought joy and happiness to the hearts of the freshers. Ashmit began the festivities by greeting first-year students. The event is followed by an opening address by TSS faculty coordinator Dr Mukesh Yadav. Prof. (Dr.) Pradeep Sharma, Dr Snehlata Dhaka, and Sanchari Basak, together with hundreds of students, saw the remarkable event successfully presented by TSS in succession. Dr Mukesh Yadav emphasized the English Department's goal and motivation in their educational program. The student's courses, jobs, and possibilities were painstakingly outlined. He introduced everyone to The Speech Society's founder, Alok Agrawal, and co-founder, Somen Halder, and appreciated everyone for coming up with such innovative ideas. He also shared his life experiences with the newcomers. He encouraged all students, regardless of family or location, to be open in front of others. They must now seize the opportunity to be an excellent speaker. He also introduced everyone to Rimi Majumdar (now in her second year of B. Tech), who was one of a few first-year students whose research papers were featured in ICALES 2022. IIT Research Scholars and Professors commended her for finishing her research report in her first semester. She thanked Dr Mukesh Yadav for his encouragement and support in allowing her to present in front of all specialists. Students expressed an interest in honing their public speaking abilities. The TSS faculty coordinator announced the position offer of Toastmasters, which will be combined into TSS and led by the English department

The day has finally arrived for Alok to take the stage and describe TSS's adventure. The TSS journey encompasses the cause for its being, the road was taken to make it a reality, and all preceding events. The event trip of Aarambh (Inauguration Event), where a popular Youtuber and Influencer Kuldeep Singhania was invited, was the most joyful time. He mingled with the newcomers and encouraged them to present themselves on stage at random. Sagnik Dey received a good reception from the audience throughout the event. Some students were requested to share their thoughts and experiences on the topics raised by Alok.

The interactive session began with TSS organizing an Improv Game for everyone. The fundamental premise of the game was to reply only by asking questions to your partner. The person who responds in a sentence will lose 1 point for the opponent. Everyone liked the talk as the game began. The game's primary goal was to break the ice and boost involvement among batchmates. The ceremony drew to a close, and awards were given out. Sayantani Dey won the game, and participants Stuti Modak, Shibagni Mahajan, Arka Mahajan, and Anjishnu Mallick got honours. Sagnik Dey and Rahul Yadav were among those who interacted the most during the event and were surprised with gifts from TSS faculty coordinator Dr Mukesh Yadav, Assistant Professor Snehlata Dhaka, and Sanchari Basak.

Finally, Ms Sanchari Basak was invited to the platform for the Vote of Thanks, where she thanked TSS for organizing such a fantastic event and the freshers for making it a success. Everyone was pleased with the outcome of the event.

The International Women's Day celebration at UEM Jaipur, organized by The Speech Society, was a resounding success. It brought together people from all walks of life to honour and appreciate the invaluable contributions of women in shaping our world. It served as a catalyst for change, inspiring individuals to take action and create a future where every woman's voice is heard and celebrated.
