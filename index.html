About University of Engineering and Management

Established in 2011, the University of Engineering and Management (UEM), Jaipur is a private university approved by AICTE, UGC and ICRA. It is an integral part of the IEM Education Trust and majorly focuses on the field of Engineering & Technology and Business & Management Studies. UEM Jaipur has been established by the IEM group, Kolkata which is an acclaimed educational group amongst the industry-centred academic training organisations. The institute has achieved the awards of “Best Private University in North India” and “Best Emerging University in North India”. UEM Jaipur offers more than 13-degree p rogrammes at UG, PG and doctoral levels in various specialisations across the streams of Engineering and Management.

About The Speech Society

The Speech Society intends to change the meaning of F.E.A.R from ‘Forget Everything And Run’ to ‘Face Everything And Rise’. We will work alongside the motto - ‘Stand Up. Step Up. Speak Up’ throughout our journey. We wish to achieve this by developing confident and fluent speakers. To bring in a perfect and good speaking culture, Alok Agarwal (Founder) and Somen Halder (Co-Founder) started ‘The Speech Society(TSS)'. TSS was started as an initiative to serve as a platform to sharpen one’s communication skills. We plan on conducting a variety of activities such as debate, elocution, spell bee, anchoring, poetry, etc.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is The Speech Society?

    THE SPEECH SOCIETY, aka, TSS is created by a group of first year students of the renowned university, UEM, Jaipur. To bring in a perfect and good speaking culture, Alok Agarwal (Founder) and Somen Halder (Co-Founder) started The Speech Society (TSS)'. TSS was started as an initiative to serve as a platform to sharpen one's communication skills.

  • Why The Speech Society ?

    The Speech Society aims to hone the speaking, listening, reading, and writing skills of all the students of UEMJ. It also strives to develop a love for the English language, while polishing the oratory skills of the students. The Speech Society intends to change the meaning of F.E.A.R from 'Forget Everything And Run' to 'Face Everything And Rise'. We will work alongside the motto-Stand Up. Step Up. Speak Up' throughout our journey.

  • What is Our Vision?

    We want to make every Uemian a good public speaker. The Speech Society aims to hone the speaking, listening, reading, and writing skills of all the students of UEMJ. It also strives to develop a love for the English language, while polishing the oratory skills of the students. In this society, students would be taught how to communicate and present their views in an appropriate format. The students would also be given ample opportunities to polish their oratory skills through a plethora of activities like extempore, debate, discussion, talk shows and a lot more.
